    Escalation protocol
    How SPEAK Up BC responds when schools fail to act within defined timeframes
    ⚠
    48-hour light warning
    If no initial response is recorded within 48 hours of a report being forwarded, a light warning is sent to the school administrator and district equity office.
    Triggers at 48 hours — school + district notified
    🚨
    10-day heavy escalation
    If a case remains unaddressed after 10 business days, a formal heavy warning is issued to the district superintendent and BC Ministry of Education equity office.
    Triggers at 10 business days — district + Ministry flagged
    🚔
    RCMP emergency escalation
    For cases involving a dangerous or threatening outcome. Staff-initiated only from the case detail view.
    Manual trigger — staff-initiated only
    Timers apply from the moment a report is forwarded to the school.
