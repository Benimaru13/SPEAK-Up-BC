# SPEAK Up BC 🗣️
### Student Platform for Equity, Accountability & Knowledge

> Built by students. For students. | YEEP Hackathon 2026 — 🏆 First Place

SPEAK Up BC is a nonprofit web platform that empowers racialized BC high school 
students to confidentially report racist incidents and track their cases to resolution 
— holding schools accountable every step of the way.

---

## The Problem

- 1 in 3 BC high school students experienced racism at school in 2023
- 46% of racialized students face discrimination in school settings
- 0 dedicated BC-wide tools existed to track school accountability

Existing reporting tools accept submissions but offer no follow-through. Reports 
disappear. Schools face no consequences for inaction. Students have no way to know 
if anything was done.

---

## The Solution

Two interconnected interfaces:

**Student Portal** — students can:
- Submit a confidential incident report (type, location, school, date, description, evidence)
- Receive a unique tracking code instantly
- Monitor their case through 4 clear stages: Received → Under Review → Action Taken → Resolved

**Admin Portal** — SPEAK Up BC staff can:
- Review incoming reports in a live case inbox
- Log detailed action updates to the case timeline
- Trigger automatic escalation warnings (48-hour and 10-day thresholds)
- Escalate dangerous cases to RCMP via an in-portal modal

---

## Accountability Mechanism

| Threshold | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 48 hours | Light warning sent to school + district equity office |
| 10 business days | Heavy escalation to district superintendent + BC Ministry of Education |
| Dangerous outcome | Manual RCMP escalation by SPEAK Up BC staff |
